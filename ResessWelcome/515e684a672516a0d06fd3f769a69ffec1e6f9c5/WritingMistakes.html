    Common Writing Mistakes

    After you have completed writing your document and ready to proofread it, please check for the common mistakes and correct, if any.

    1. Do not forget to insert a whitespace between a word and "(".
        Share your calendar(Google calendar).
        Share your calendar (Google calendar).
    2. Use Oxford comma style to avoid ambiguity.
        I love my parents, Eminem and Lady Gaga.
        I love my parents, Eminem, and Lady Gaga.
    3. Do not use passive voice, especially with ambiguous actors.
        The work was completed in time.
        I completed the work in time.
    4. Do not use citations as nouns.
        [1] shows.
        Eminem and Lady Gaga [1] show.
        Bad Meets Evil [1] shows.
    5. Insert a space before citations.
        Eminem and Lady Gaga[1] show.
        Eminem and Lady Gaga [1] show,
    6. Be explicit. If your statement can be copied to any other text exactly as it is, then it is a bad statement.
        "Interesting and unique perspective on the problem" --> clearly state what is that perspective exactly?
        "They identified main motivations for developers to do CD and main CD tools used" --> what are these motivations and tools?
    7. Numbers below 10 are spelled out, numbers starting from 10 are given as numbers. Exceptions are when you have a series of numbers or numbers have "special" meaning.
        "We performed 10, 4, 6, and 12 runs" is correct, as that is a series.
        "The machine was 1GB of memory" is accaptable as these numbers are often presented in such a form.
        "We interviewed 3 people" is not acceptable.
    8. Bullet points should be consistent in structure (verbs vs. nouns, punctuation, etc.).
    9. Always include slide numbers.
    10. Fonts in presentations should always be at least 20 points.
    11. Avoid contracted forms in all formal text, especially scientific content.
        don't, can't, let's.
        do not, cannot, let us.
    12. Do not use "this", "it", etc. to avoid ambiguity.
        The city council denied the protesters a permit because they feared violence.
        The city council denied the protesters a permit because they advocated violence.
        The hat fit in the box because it was big.
        The hat didn't fit in the box because it was big.
